# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

People evaluating architecture services through a public-facing brand site. They are typically considering a residential, commercial, facade or inspiration-led project and want clarity, trust, and a sense that the studio understands their space before the first contact.

## Product Purpose

Present Marcia Nath as a clear, coherent architecture brand and convert interest into qualified contact. The site should help a visitor understand the studio's point of view, the kinds of projects it handles, and the next step to start a briefing.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, consultative. The voice should feel confident without sounding promotional, with copy that emphasizes decision quality, property value, and day-to-day function.

## Anti-references

Generic portfolio filler, repetitive marketing claims, unexplained jargon, overly decorative layouts, and copy that treats all project types as if they were the same.

## Design Principles

Lead with the project's value to the client, keep each page coherent with its topic, use language that supports decision-making, and preserve a refined architectural tone across languages.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Keep the site readable at mobile and desktop sizes, preserve clear navigation, support reduced motion where relevant, and maintain translated labels and content for the selected language.
